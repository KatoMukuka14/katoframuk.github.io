About Us
Monita Electro Technology limited was formed in January 2011. It is an ICT company Specialized in providing IT solutions to business firms, offering new state of art technologies that we monitor in behalf of our clients and partners. our main business is to reduce or prevent fraud activities in trading places by offering a variety of point of sale softwares, securing business places with Access Control Systems and monitoring the places which CCTV system. SECURITY SYSTEMS
Monita Technologies installs Digital video Recorder system. It uses a secure fire proof computers (DVR) record video data and integrated access control events as digital timed data for easy play back and multi monitoring points since it can be install on any IP support network. It supports both wireless and ordinary Cameras.
ACCESS CONTROL SYSTEMS
We install and provide support services for different types of access control systems, like Impro range of access control, softcon access control, Sagem Biometric system and HID systems. According to our customer requirements, we are able select a system that will meet there desire security system. With access control, it prevents or control movements of both employees and visitors on site at the same time recording every event on security doors and gates.
FLEET MANAGEMENT AND FUEL CONTROL
NETPOS/NETACC SYSTEM
This is a system that enables you to control and monitor fuel dispensing. It can be installed at a commercial filling station as a point of sale or industrial fuel pumps for fuel management and control.
ELECTRONIC TANK GAUGE SYSTEM
Different types of liquids stored in tanks can be monitored and measured using Electronic Tank Gauge (ETG) and have every event on the computer. At any time reports can be generated without dipping and manual volume calculations.
